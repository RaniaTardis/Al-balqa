About Balqa Tourism

Welcome to Balqa Tourism, your guide to exploring the heart of Jordan’s rich heritage and natural beauty. Nestled in the Balqa Governorate, our region is a treasure trove of historical landmarks, vibrant cultural traditions, and breathtaking landscapes. From the sacred Baptism Site (Al-Maghtas) to the bustling Hammam Street in Salt and the ancient Roman Tombs in Wadi Shuaib, Balqa offers an authentic journey through time and tradition.

At Balqa Tourism, we are passionate about showcasing the soul of our region. Our mission is to connect visitors with the local communities, their stories, and their traditions through sustainable and immersive experiences. Whether you’re strolling through the historic streets of As-Salt, a UNESCO World Heritage Site celebrated for its tolerance and urban hospitality, or savoring local cuisine at a family-run farm, we invite you to discover the warmth and diversity of Balqa.

Our Commitment

Authentic Experiences

Engage with local artisans, farmers, and communities through unique activities like cooking classes, cultural tours, and agritourism adventures, inspired by initiatives like Bookagri.

Sustainable Tourism

We prioritize eco-friendly practices and support local economies, ensuring your visit preserves Balqa’s natural and cultural heritage for future generations.

Community Connection

Our tours and experiences empower local women, farmers, and youth, fostering economic growth and cultural pride in Balqa Governorate.

Why Visit Balqa?

Balqa is more than a destination—it’s an experience that blends spirituality, history, and community. Wander through the ancient streets of As-Salt, explore the serene Jordan River, or immerse yourself in the vibrant markets and local crafts. With Balqa Tourism, every step is a story, and every visit supports the heart of Jordan’s cultural legacy.
